      How to Start Investing

      Learn the basics of investing and follow these steps to begin safely:

      1. Set Financial Goals: Define short-term and long-term goals.
      2. Determine Budget: Decide how much you can invest safely.
      3. Learn Investment Types: Explore stocks, bonds, ETFs, and funds.
      4. Open an Account: Choose a brokerage or investment app.
      5. Start Small: Make small, diversified investments.
      6. Monitor & Adjust: Track your portfolio and rebalance as needed.

      Quick Tips:

      • Build an emergency fund before investing.
      • Diversify to reduce risk.
      • Invest only money you can afford to lock away.
      • Think long-term; avoid emotional decisions.
      • Understand risk vs reward.

      About Budget Wise

      Welcome to our website!

      Living in Cape Town has become more expensive day by day. From higher housing costs, groceries, and electricity bills, getting lost is easy. Our vision is to enable you to be in control of your finances by breaking down budgeting to make it relevant and even fun.

      This is what we can do for you:

      Budget Smarter: Track your income and expenditure, know where your money is being spent, and get recommended budgeting breakdowns based on your lifestyle.

      Save Money While You Shop: Our AI technology captures prices at stores, finds coupons, and identifies bundle prices to enable you to spend less without sacrificing quality.

      Engage & Be Rewarded: Finish tasks, stay within budgets, and implement saving tips to earn points. Redeem points for custom themes or shopping vouchers, which makes saving engaging and fun.

      Invest for the Future:Receive investment tips for starters and overall investment advice to maximize savings and gain financial security.

      Our system is adaptable and convenient. You can handle budgets, track spending, and make sounder financial choices, all in one place, whether you are a student, a professional, or someone juggling a family.
